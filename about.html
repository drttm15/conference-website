About the Conference

The International Conference on Emerging Trends 2025 serves as a premier platform to explore advancements in wireless and mobile communication technologies. Key focus areas include 5G networks, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, and innovations in microwave and millimeter-wave engineering. These sessions aim to highlight groundbreaking research that supports sustainable technological development.

The conference emphasizes state-of-the-art work in signal and image processing, medical imaging, antenna design, RF propagation, and computational electromagnetics. It fosters dialogue around critical communication technologies, offering a rich environment for exchanging novel ideas in these rapidly evolving fields.

In addition, the conference explores a wide range of computing technologies, including cloud computing, big data analytics, bioinformatics, data mining, distributed systems, cryptography, and cybersecurity. These interdisciplinary topics reflect the complexities and challenges of the digital era, encouraging integrated solutions across multiple domains.

The energy sector is another major focus, with sessions dedicated to electric power systems, renewable energy integration, energy storage, efficiency optimization, smart grid infrastructure, and advanced power semiconductor devices. By bringing together researchers, industry experts, and academics worldwide, ICET 2025 aims to promote innovation, knowledge sharing, and sustainable solutions for future technological growth.
