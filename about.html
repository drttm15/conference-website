About the Conference

This conference serves as a distinguished platform for delving into the forefront of wireless and mobile communication technologies, with an emphasis on 5G, reconfigurable intelligent networks, and advancements in microwave and millimeter-wave engineering. It underscores cutting-edge, sustainable research across signal and image processing, medical imaging innovations, antenna design, RF propagation, and electromagnetic computation. The event further explores critical domains such as network planning, capacity optimization, cognitive radio, dynamic spectrum allocation, and satellite communication systems. In the sphere of energy, the conference addresses transformative trends in electric power systems, renewable energy integration, energy storage solutions, efficiency optimization, electrified transportation, smart grid infrastructures, and next-generation power semiconductor devices. A core emphasis is placed on fostering the synergy between technological innovation and sustainable development. By convening leading researchers, industry experts, and academicians from around the globe, this conference aims to facilitate profound knowledge exchange and catalyze collaborations that will shape the trajectory of future-defining technologies.
