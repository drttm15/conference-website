About the Conference

This conference serves as a distinguished platform for delving into the forefront of wireless and mobile communication technologies, with an emphasis on 5G, reconfigurable intelligent networks, and advancements in microwave and millimeter-wave engineering. It underscores cutting-edge, sustainable research across signal and image processing, medical imaging innovations, antenna design, RF propagation, and electromagnetic computation.
